Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Restro Grow uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the service to function properly, including authentication and session management
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our service to improve performance
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor system performance and identify issues

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. These are essential for maintaining your login session and ensuring security.

Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. These remember your preferences and settings.

Third-Party Cookies: Set by third-party services we use, such as payment processors or analytics tools.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify settings to decline them
  • Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our service
  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the service to work

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These include payment processors, analytics services, and other tools. We do not control these cookies, and you should review the privacy policies of these third parties.

6. Cookie Consent

By using our service, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree to our use of cookies, please adjust your browser settings accordingly or discontinue use of our service.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page.
